About us

Focal Resto Lounge strives to be the spot to go for the local neighborhood as well as being a destination location for people from other parts of the city. We bring this to you by providing a menu that is familiar to people but with a global twist by incorporating global ingredients and spices into dishes . Having been in the food industry for many years we have only gotten better with time like the fine wine we will be serving you.


A modern restaurant with a touch of the rustic charm enhanced by our friendly staff, globally diverse menu, and whole new drink selections that'll widen the horizon of your taste buds, is what's waiting for you at Focal Resto Lounge. At Queen you can feel like a Queen or a King with easy access to a fine dining experience at an affordable price without a hit to your wallet so that you can keep coming back for more!




Bringing you great value, outstanding service, beautiful atmosphere and overall a memorable dining experience is our focus.
